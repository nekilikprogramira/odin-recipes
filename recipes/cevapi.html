Cevapi

Description:

It's cevapi

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Bread(Lepina)Beef
  • Onions

Steps:

  1. Mix beef with salt and eggs
  2. Take the beef and make it look like the letter I and make many of them
  3. Cook them either on the grill or a pan
  4. Serve it with Lepina, onions and maybe yogurt
